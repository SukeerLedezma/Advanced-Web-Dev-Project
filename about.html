About Us

Welcome to Dulce Café, your go-to destination for delightful Mexican coffee and sweets. Our cafe was founded in the heart of Connecticut, where we've been serving the community with passion and dedication for years. At Dulce Café, we take pride in bringing the authentic flavors of Mexico to West Haven, CT, offering a cozy and inviting atmosphere where you can relax and indulge in your favorite treats.

Whether you're craving a piping hot cup of coffee to kickstart your day or a sweet pastry to satisfy your cravings, our menu has something for everyone. From traditional Mexican beverages to mouthwatering desserts, each item is crafted with care using the finest ingredients, ensuring a memorable experience with every visit.
